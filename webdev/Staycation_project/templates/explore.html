Explore Mount Timpanogos

🥾 Hiking Trails

Mount Timpanogos offers several popular trails, including the Timpooneke Trail and the Aspen Grove Trail. These routes take hikers through forests, meadows, and rocky slopes before reaching the summit. Along the way, you’ll encounter waterfalls, wildlife, and incredible scenic views.

🏞️ Attractions & Landmarks

  • Emerald Lake near the summit
  • Timpanogos Cave National Monument
  • Scenic waterfalls along the trails
  • The historic bomber crash site
  • High mountain viewpoints with panoramic views

🦌 Wildlife

The mountain is home to a variety of wildlife including mountain goats, deer, marmots, and birds of prey. Early morning hikers often have the best chance to spot animals in their natural habitat.

🌄 Best Times to Visit

Summer is the most popular time to hike, with wildflowers covering the mountain and clear trails leading to the summit. Fall brings stunning colors and fewer crowds, while winter offers a more challenging but peaceful snow-covered landscape. Sunset and sunrise are especially beautiful times to experience the mountain.
